Clockwork Servitors

Azlanti Replay Device - This clockwork bust of a nondescript woman has a small slot built into the forehead the same size and shape as an Azlanti Recording Gem. If a gem is slotted in the contraption's forehead, it replays the information stored within, projecting any visual information via its eyes while its mouth moves along with produced sounds.

Clockwork Spy - Self-winding, unknown orders, captured.

Unique Magical Items

Azlanti Timepiece - Wound, working, and at correct time. Must be wound every 60 days. Also includes compass. Possible precursor to Wayfinders.

Azlanti Longsword - Inscibed with the name "Xiphos" this +1 Azlanti Longsword is immune to the wear and rust of even water or time. It may yet have undiscovered properties.

Water or Surface Exploration Equipment

Rowboat - Seats up to 6 people.

Canoe - Seats up to 3 people.

Trophies on Display

Ankheg Fang - Harvested by Morogak after slaying a Mother Ankheg in Talmandor's Bounty.

Boar Tusk - Harvested by Morogak after figthing wild boar east of Talmandor's Bounty.

Social Favors

Faedwyr Trunclebrook - He always backs the expedition team 100%, granting each PC one additional social round during colony-wide events and social crisis' he is a member in as he helps keep the crowds patient and willing to listen.

Gaege Murani - Her motherly insight grants a +2 competence bonus on influence and discovery checks against children, youth, and small creatures.

Tipps Kreggan - Gifts an expedition teamster a rock or gem each week that Tipps found and reminded Tipps of the teamster. GM rolls to determine value and teamster.
