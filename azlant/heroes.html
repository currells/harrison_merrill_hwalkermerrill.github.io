Talmandor's Bounty Colony Charter

Colony Status: Growing

Current Assignment

After several months in Ancorato, the colonists from the first wave have been rescued, reinforcemnets have arrived, and the colony is growing at a brisk pace. While many are cheerfully continuing to build Talmandor's Bounty, the disturbing revelations of late have caused others to be fearful and doubtful. The aboleth Onthooth had been sending his servants to raid the colony for test subject stock and many of the original colonists are dead. And while Onthooth was slain by the expedition team, Onthooth alluded to serving someone greater - a veiled master - who seems to be searching through Azlanti Ruins for an ancient super-weapon.

    Standing Orders:
  • Save the lives of Colony Leadership, Expedition Members, Shopkeeps and Colonists in Good Standing, Security Forces, and all other intelligent life, where possible, and in that order.
  • Do not take unecessary risks with the lives of expedition team members or colonists.
  • Make all reasonable attempts towards peaceful contact with intelligent local life, where peace is possible, without failing to defend the colony or the expedition team.
  • Make all reasonable attempts to establish peaceful diplomatic and trade relations with intelligent local life to support the colony's stability, development, and expansion.
  • Discover, Explore, Record, and Report on landmarks, locations of interest, and azlanti ruins in and around Ancorato.
    Current Assignments:
  • Reach the underwater city of Talasantri and establish (peaceful) contact.
  • Set up trade relations with merchants in the city.
  • Meet with Talasantri's Learder, Rillkimatai, and request assitance.
  • Locate and recover the ancient Azlanti Super-Weapon

Color Code: Blue = Helpful; Light Green = Friendly; White = Indifferent; Light Red = Unfriendly; Black = Hostile - Approach with Caution
