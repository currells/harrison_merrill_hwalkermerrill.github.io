Repetition

Company Name

FreshBooks


How Used

Repetition is a powerful principle of design that correlates with how repeating certain elements can help to organize information. By repeating elements throughout a webpage or website, such as using identical headers and repeating fonts, colors, font sizes, styles, alignment, and other elements, messages can be reemphasized, and elements can be tied together. In the screenshot provided by FreshBooks, repetition is used in matching fonts and colors for the section titles, similarly sized, colored, and organized photos, and matching body text font, color, and alignment. By following the rules of repetition, a webpage can become easier to read, it becomes much more attractive, it can avoid the pitfalls of looking “too busy” or “too messy”, and it can emphasize and reemphasize information easily.
