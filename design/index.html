Proximity

Company Name

Proximity Capital Partners


How Used

Proximity is the principle of design that correlates to how the organization of information we see can help to group things together. At the top of the screenshot from Proximity Capital Partners, you can see how their logo is a perfect example of this. With one hexagon broken off from the rest, it appears separate and distinct from the others, even though it could obviously fit. Similarly, the words grouped within the picture are separated from the main body by the “About” header. This separates the concise statement within the image from the main information on the site. By following the rules of proximity, the website is easier and more pleasant to read, while also helping the reader to better find, group, associate, and retain the presented information.
