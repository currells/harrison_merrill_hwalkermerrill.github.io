    Licenses collected or still required for the purchase of restricted goods
  • Aquired - Freedom Papers - Allows play of enslaved races: Halfling, Gnome, Catfolk, Goblin, Hobgoblin.
  • Aquired - Craftsman License - Allows the sale of personally created and signed mundane items.
  • Aquired - Right to Personal Protection - Allows wear of light armor, shields, and one handed weapons, and may wear and carry in public.
  • Aquired - Right to Defense of Property - Allows the purchase of non-reach two handed weapons, but may not carry in public.
  • Aquired - Right to Arcane and Divine - Allows the casting of spells, but not in public.
  • Aquired - Guard License - Allows the purchase and public carry of two handed reach weapons and medium and heavy armor.
  • Aquired - Specialist License - Allows the purchase and sale of Masterwork items and items made from special materials.
  • Aquired - Enchanters License - Allows the creation and sale of personally enchanted and signed magical items.
  • Aquired - Component License - Allows the purchase and sale of potions, scrolls, and alchemical and alchemically-treated items.
  • Denied - Casting License - Allows the casting of spells in public.
  • Denied - Special Operator x - Allows the purchase, sale, and public carry of magical weapons and armor up to +x.
  • Denied - Aristocracy Permit - Allows the purchase, sale, and public carry of wonderous items and magical rings.
  • Denied - Arcanist Permit - Allows the purchase, sale, and public carry of rods, staves, and wands.
  • Denied - Crenelation Authorization - Allows the enchanting and abjuration of personal property, the purchase and creation of constructs, and the hiring of guards.
  • Denied - Merchant Marine - Allows the purchase, sale, and public carry of any non-contraband item.
  • Denied - Hellknight Authority - Allows the purchase, sale, and public carry of any item with no restrictions.
